 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW AdParlor, a division of Adknowledge, provides an industry leading software as a service platform that enables the world's largest advertisers to efficiently manage and optimize their social advertising spend on Facebook, Twitter, and other social marketplaces. Our team is currently building the next generation of our platform and looking for experienced individuals with a history of delivering reliable software while meeting tight deadlines. The AdParlor business has a strong technology infrastructure, consisting wholly of proprietary code developed for the following functions: Consuming and interfacing with Facebook, Twitter and other social ad platform APIs An easy-to-use client-facing interface with flexible reporting and permissions Running calculations and automating decisions to determine the optimal cohorts of users and the appropriate bid for each cohort A robust tracking system designed to track return on investment and lifetime value of users for our advertisers  Team The Software Engineering team is located in downtown Toronto and is comprised of backend engineers, frontend engineers, product designers, and product managers. The Senior Software Engineer position will work closely with the backend development team and reports directly to the Vice President of Engineering.  POSITION OVERVIEW   The Engineering Team Lead is responsible for helping define the future of our SPA and multi-API intercommunication platform. A typical day has a healthy mix of working closely with the product team to help define new features, building and improving product features, writing unit tests against these features, and fixing problems identified by our stakeholders. Our platform utilizes many different languages and technologies including Ruby, Resqueue, RabbitMQ, PHP, NodeJS, Java, MySQL, and MongoDB.  Primary duties include:  1. Collaborating with peers on architectural design and implementation 2. Planning the implementation of new Facebook features and interacting with Facebook contacts to give feedback on beta features and functionality.
