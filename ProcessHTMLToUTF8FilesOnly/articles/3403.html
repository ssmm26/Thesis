POSITION SUMMARY


Reporting directly to VTRAC\u2019s Sr. Manager, Client Services and working on a growing team of technical recruitment specialists, you will be primarily responsible for applying your well-developed search and headhunting skills to identify, engage and secure quality IT talents in shortest possible time. In addition, you will be equally accountable for validating those candidate\u2019s interests, true skills and competencies with your strong technical recruiting background.

\u00a0

The primary functions of this role consist of:

  • Seeking to understand full scope of staffing requirements for supporting business lines and clients.

  • Manage full cycle recruitment and staffing objectives for both contract and permanent placement opportunities.

  • Meeting and interviewing candidates to understand their interest, motivation, career plan, technical and behavioural skills. Act as an advisor on prospective candidates.

  • Actively engage in technical, behavioural or situational interviewing assessments.

  • Preparing qualified slate of prospective candidates to businesses along with supporting recommendations.

  • Working with our corporate recruitment partners/clients to ensure the offer negotiation and process is optimized and operating within the constraints of our client\u2019s standards, protocols and expectations.

  • Support and supervise other recruiters by imparting your knowledge, experience and skills with the objective of ensuring that VTRAC operates at high-performing levels.

  • Recruit senior IT and finance professionals

  • Co-ordinate and participate in selection and presentation of Candidates to prospective LOB\u2019s and departments.

  • Coordinate and manage relationship with clients, including position evaluation, presentation, negotiation,\u00a0 placement, performance follow up and potential termination of the placed candidates.\u00a0

    \u00a0

    \u00a0

    ESSENTIAL SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS

  • Minimum four (4) years\u2019 experience with a successful track record of recruitment supporting IT departments of high tech, financial, manufacturing in a high-performing and rapidly changing professional services environment.

  • Demonstrated achievement in recruiting for hard-to-find talent including but not limited to development platforms, application development, architecture (all domains), security, business intelligence, infrastructure and project management for a variety of industry verticals such as banking and investments/wealth Management, public sector/utilities, management consulting and telecommunications.

  • Experience in recruitment and placing Senior IT professionals, and managing clients.

  • Experience in recruitment of IT professionals in technical environment such as Java, Unix, Oracle, .Net, ETL (SAS or Informatica).

  • Strong practical experience working with Ms. Office, especially with Word.

  • Demonstrated competency in working with competing priorities.

  • Experience in project-based IT staffing, from negotiations to closing with both candidates and clients.

  • Experience in providing consultative advice to candidates.

  • Ability to successfully navigate complex, matrix-based organizational.

  • Very strong team player.

  • Bachelor or College Degree/Diploma in Psychology, Computer Science, Business, Commerce or Human Resources or four years of equal experience.
