Senior Analyst Developers

  • two permanent

\u00a0

If you are an IT professional with a strong technical background along with excellent analytical and client service skills, here is your opportunity to utilize your Siebel/Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems knowledge and project leadership skills.

\u00a0

What can I expect to do in this role?

You will:

  • work collaboratively with client managers and staff stakeholders to ensure that client business needs are understood and addressed
  • provide technical systems development expertise by coordinating analysis of client needs
  • provide technical expertise in complex systems analysis and design
  • plan, coordinate and lead the technical design, development, implementation and/or maintenance of technical solutions and core applications including overseeing all phases of projects
  • provide consultation, training and technical advice
  • lead and/or participate on project teams engaged in a wide variety of I&IT development projects

\u00a0

Location: 777 Bay Street, Toronto, Ontario

\u00a0

How do I qualify?

Systems Knowledge:

  • you have knowledge of and skills in Siebel/CRM application administration, installing, uninstalling and managing Siebel/CRM servers (web, Application, Gateway, Siebel/CRM Database Servers, Reports and Remote Servers)
  • you have knowledge of and skills in performance tuning, troubleshooting, production monitoring, maintenance, support, configuration, performance and analysis tuning of a multi-tiered Siebel environment
  • you have knowledge of Siebel/CRM configuration using Siebel tools, Siebel Remote, Siebel Anywhere, Siebel Workflow, Siebel EIM, Siebel Reports development and administration, Siebel data model and object model

\u00a0

Technical Knowledge:

  • you demonstrate knowledge of systems development life cycle (SDLC), theory and principles of systems analysis and design, development, related methods and techniques
  • you demonstrate knowledge of PC and related technologies, software and concepts including operating system platforms, application and database management software, programming languages, scripting and servlets (e.g. PERL, VB Script, XML, JAVA, ASP, EJB, J2EE), application support (e.g. B2B, B2C), databases (e.g. Oracle), servers (e.g. Compact, IBM, SUN)
  • you have knowledge of and skills in the development and support of large applications including enterprise technology platforms, system technologies, I&IT architectures, content management software, database management systems and application database administration, relational database technology and query languages, programming languages and concepts, Internet, web services and emerging technologies for business applications
  • you have knowledge of quality assurance process, methodology and tools, quality assurance testing and implementation methods and practices to design testing procedures

\u00a0

Project Management Skills:

  • you have knowledge of project management techniques and methodology including chartering, planning, estimating, budgeting, scheduling, measuring, monitoring and controlling to manage large and complex application projects and concurrent project components

\u00a0

Analytical, Problem-Solving and Evaluation Skills:

  • you have analytical and problem-solving skills to analyze and evaluate design alternatives and consider business needs and lead the development of I&IT solutions to improve program and service delivery
  • your evaluation skills enable you to conceptualize approaches to monitor and evaluate the efficiency and effectiveness of applications managed by the Branch

\u00a0

Communication Skills:

  • you have oral and written communication and presentation skills to discuss and explain analyses and recommendations, prepare business cases and develop technical documentation
