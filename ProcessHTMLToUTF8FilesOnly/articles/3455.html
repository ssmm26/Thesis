Description:
As the Senior Software Performance Analyst, you will design, develop and execute software performance and scalability tests for Open Text products. You will analyze the results of these tests, offer diagnosis of bottlenecks, and optimize their design and deployment.

These tests will reflect both realistic usage profiles and data volumes expected in the field as well as stress tests that push the limits of the product\u2019s capabilities on a variety of platforms and infrastructures. The tests developed should offer a fine granularity of timing breakdowns in order to build up a code profile of where time is being spent.

Once measurements and baselines are taken, you will analyze slow operations and assess their efficiency, producing findings reports or whitepapers. You will work with other members of the Performance Engineering team and respective Open Text product development teams, to share an understanding of issues found, propose alternative approaches, enhancements, or infrastructure and configuration best practices to optimize product performance.

In addition to the actual testing and analysis, you will setup, configure and tune the performance lab equipment and infrastructure software to support these tests.

You will work with and direct the efforts of other Performance Analysts, using non-functional Agile methodologies to manage and lead projects.
